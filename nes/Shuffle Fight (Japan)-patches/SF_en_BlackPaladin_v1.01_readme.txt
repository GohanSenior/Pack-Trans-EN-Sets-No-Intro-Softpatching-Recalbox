*Shuffle Fight*
***************

Shuffle Fight for the NES is a boardgame simulator, released by Banpresto, with the focus of various giant robots engaged in a board/card game.  Despite having super robots used in the game, this is not a part of the Super Robot Wars franchise.  It's a part of the Compati Hero francise.  
The following super robots are represented in this game...

Mobile Suit Gundam 0080: War in the Pocket
Mobile Suit Zeta Gundam
Mobile Suit ZZ Gundam

Combat Mecha Xabungle
Heavy Metal L-Gaim

Mazinger Z
Great Mazinger

Getter Robo G
Fighting General Daimos
Future Robot Daltanious
