
Tetris Battle Spin Off

Translation Patch (Version 1)


-----------------------
  GENERAL INFORMATION
-----------------------

Filename:  Tetris Battle Gaiden (Japan)
File size: 1.00 MB (1,048,576 bytes)
CRC32:     B8F5F846


This patch is for Tetris Battle Spin Off on the Super Famicom.
The text has been translated from Japanese into American English.

Two patching formats are provided, so you can use either .bps or .ips.
(Do not apply both formats of patches.)

The target Japanese ROM must have a size of 1.00 MB (1,048,576 bytes),
and have a CRC32 checksum of B8F5F846.
If not, the patch will not work as intended.
